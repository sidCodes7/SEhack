### ✅ Confirmed Enhancements (Additions to original PS)

**[WF#2] Live Heatmap Demo Moment**
_Concept:_ Submit a live issue during the demo → heatmap dot appears on campus map in real time → ticket routes to support team visibly. Zero explanation needed.
_Novelty:_ The "aha moment" is self-demonstrating. The product explains itself.

**[WF#4] AI Copilot with Semester Memory (Proactive Guardian)**
_Concept:_ Copilot proactively surfaces: "Your scholarship window closes in 2 days. You have 2 pending steps." — without the student asking.
_Novelty:_ Transforms Q&A bot into a personal campus guardian. "This app knows me."

**[WF#5] AI Copilot — Mother Tongue Support**
_Concept:_ Multilingual responses (Hindi, Tamil, Marathi etc.) for first-gen students navigating bureaucratic processes.
_Novelty:_ India-specific differentiation + deep accessibility story. Emotional resonance with judges.

**[WF#6] Notice → Copilot Awareness Loop**
_Concept:_ Professor publishes a notice → Copilot auto-ingests it → can answer student questions from it instantly. "When is the assignment due?" answered from live data.
_Novelty:_ Real-time knowledge loop between professor workflow and student AI. Closes the communication silo.

**[WF#7] Ghost Mode — Predictive Auto-Approval**
_Concept:_ System learns booking patterns → pre-approves routine requests before submission. Student gets "pre-approved slot" notification.
_Novelty:_ Inverts the approval paradigm. System waits for the student, not the other way around.

**[WF#8] Swiggy-Style Approval Progress Bar**
_Concept:_ Real-time visual tracker: "HoD ✅ → Stucco 🕐 → Dean pending." Student knows exactly who is holding up their request.
_Novelty:_ Makes accountability transparent. Eliminates the "request black hole" experience.

**[WF#10] Campus Karma Score**
_Concept:_ Reporting issues, attending class, returning rooms early = karma points. High-karma students get priority booking slots.
_Novelty:_ Gamification of civic responsibility. Turns good behaviour into a visible, rewarding metric.

**[WF#13] Live Plugin Demo (Super App in Action)**
_Concept:_ During the hackathon demo, "install" a mock canteen tracker mini-app into Aether in real time. Judges watch the plugin system work, not just hear about it.
_Novelty:_ Architecture becomes tangible. Most teams describe extensibility — you demonstrate it.

**[WF#14] Developer Portal — Competitors Become Collaborators**
_Concept:_ End of demo pitch: "Other teams here today can build plugins for Aether using our API." Positions Aether as a platform, not just a product.
_Novelty:_ Boldest move in the room. Reframes the entire competitive dynamic.

### 🟡 Low Priority (MAYBE)

**[WF#9] AI-Generated "Fix It" Suggestions on Rejection**
_Concept:_ When a request is rejected, the system auto-suggests alternative slots or corrective actions. One-tap resubmit.
